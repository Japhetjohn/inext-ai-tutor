First Feature (Users Dashboard Feature)
User Profile & Connectivity
Username: Displayed prominently at the top.
Connected Wallet Address: Users must connect their Web3 wallet.
Social Integration: Links to X (Twitter) and Discord.
IP Tracking: Prevents Sybil attacks by linking user profiles to their IP addresses.
Learning Progress & Analytics
Notes Section: Users can jot down key insights and track their personal learning.
Time Spent Tracking: Logs the number of hours a user has spent on the platform.
Progress Chart: Graphical representation of learning phases and completion rate.
Quiz Performance: Tracks quiz scores and completion rate after each course.
Points & Rewards Display: Users can see the points they have accumulated through engagement.
Additional Features
Leaderboard: Display top learners based on engagement and quiz performance.
Achievements & Badges: Unlock badges based on milestones reached.
Discord Community Discussion Board: A forum-like space for users to discuss topics.

Second Feature(The web3 Tutor)
The iNextAI learning model is designed to provide a structured and adaptive learning experience, catering to learners at different levels of expertise. The tutor operates in three distinct phases: Basic (Newbies Pack), Advanced (Web3 Practitioner), and Expert (Web3 Builder & Innovator).

Learning Algorithm Flow
User Selection: When a user selects a phase, a dropdown model appears with course outlines.
Course Progression: Courses are arranged in ascending order. Users must complete one module before unlocking the next.
AI Assistance: iNextAI provides step-by-step guidance, baby-step explanations, and AI-generated images where necessary.
Tracking & Assessment:
Track user progress
Generate quizzes based on completed modules
Issue points & badges for achievements
